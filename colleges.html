01

Initial Discussion (Free)

Fill out our partnership form or call us. We'll schedule a free 30-minute consultation with your T&P or placement cell head to understand your batch profile, upcoming placement season timeline, and specific challenges.

02

Custom Proposal Within 48 Hours

Based on your requirements, we'll send a detailed proposal with program recommendations, timeline, trainer profiles, and transparent pricing. No generic packages — everything is designed for you.

03

MOU Signing & Onboarding

Once you approve the proposal, we sign a simple MOU. Our onboarding team will coordinate all pre-program logistics — student communication templates, schedule announcements, and infrastructure checklist.

04

Program Delivery

Our trainers arrive on campus and deliver the program as planned. You'll receive weekly progress updates. Students attend highly engaging, practical sessions that they actually look forward to.

05

Outcome Documentation & Follow-Up

At the end of the program, we submit a complete outcome report including skill assessments, mock performance data, and placement readiness scores. We schedule a follow-up session 30 days later at no cost.
