Our Story

From BITS Pilani to Industry

We're Prashant Pandey and Utkarsh Vaish - BITS Pilani 2018 graduates. We've spent 8+ years at companies like Razorpay, PayU, Harness, StarTree, and Multiplier, working on systems that handle millions of transactions daily.

But more importantly, we've been on both sides of the interview table. We remember the stress of campus placements. And now, having interviewed hundreds of candidates ourselves, we know exactly what separates those who get hired from those who don't.

The gap between college education and industry expectations is massive. We're here to bridge it - with practical, no-nonsense training that actually prepares students for real interviews.

🎯 Our Mission

Give every student access to the same interview preparation that gets people into top tech companies - practical, real-world, no fluff.

🔭 Our Approach

We teach what actually matters in interviews - because we've conducted hundreds of them. No theory, no outdated content, just what works.

What We Stand For

Our Values Shape Everything We Do

🔨

Practical Over Theoretical

We never teach what's not immediately applicable. If students can't use it in the next interview or their first job, it's not in our curriculum.

📊

Outcomes Over Optics

No vanity metrics. We measure success by placement rates, interview conversion, and student confidence - not attendance or certificate counts.

🤝

Genuine Partnership

We treat college placement officers as partners, not clients. We're invested in their success - because that's the only way student outcomes improve.

🌍

Accessibility First

Tier 3 college students deserve the same quality of preparation as those at Tier 1. We actively work to make our programs affordable and reachable.
