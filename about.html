Our Story

Started from a Single Frustration

Career Launchpad was born in 2021, when our founder sat in on campus placement drives at a Tier 2 engineering college in Madhya Pradesh. What he witnessed was disheartening — bright, hardworking students failing interviews not because of lack of intelligence, but because nobody had ever taught them how to present themselves, how to think under pressure, or how to use the tools that companies actually use.

The curriculum hadn't changed in years. Placement cells were overwhelmed. Students were unprepared. And the industry was moving faster than ever.

We decided to build the program we wished existed. Practical. Short. High-impact. And designed to be delivered directly inside colleges — reaching students where they are.

🎯 Our Mission

Improve student employability at every college in India — regardless of tier, stream, or location.

🔭 Our Vision

A world where every college graduate is genuinely ready for the world of work — not just holding a degree, but equipped with real skills.

What We Stand For

Our Values Shape Everything We Do

🔨

Practical Over Theoretical

We never teach what's not immediately applicable. If students can't use it in the next interview or their first job, it's not in our curriculum.

📊

Outcomes Over Optics

No vanity metrics. We measure success by placement rates, interview conversion, and student confidence — not attendance or certificate counts.

🤝

Genuine Partnership

We treat college placement officers as partners, not clients. We're invested in their success — because that's the only way student outcomes improve.

🌍

Accessibility First

Tier 3 college students deserve the same quality of preparation as those at Tier 1. We actively work to make our programs affordable and reachable.
